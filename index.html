Famox supports those in need

Led by Harry Helper, Famox is an Australian-based charity dedicated to helping those in need. From global to local, Famox seeks to help all.

There are many ways in which you can help us change the world. Here in our website, we run a shop with eco-friendly and ethically made products in which anyone can purchase from! The proceeds from Famox's shop then goes towards our cause. However, if you have no interest in any of our products, then our projects and general donation page is always open. In our projects page, you can choose to donate any amount to any of our current projects. If undecided then you can allow Famox to choose where your donations go by donating to our general donations page instead.

To access Famox's website and any of its functionalities, please register for an account or login to an existing one.

As this website is a demo for our real site, we unfortunately only have admin pages at the moment. Hence why our homepage's navigation bar is populated with admin page choices with no client options as of yet.
